Tours

Organic Wineries and Olive Mills:
The purpose of this excursion is to explore the leading representatives of organic viticulture and olive cultivation in the primary wine-producing region of Mendoza (Maipú department). We will visit various wineries and olive oil mills to learn about the production processes that exclude pesticides, chemical additives, and sulfites.

Hop On & Hop Off Wine Bus:
We are the new way to explore and experience the Wine Routes of Luján de Cuyo, Maipú, and the Uco Valley. With seven weekly departures from the main hotels in the city of Mendoza, the 'International Wine Capital,' this is designed for all those who wish to immerse themselves in the wine culture and visit Wineries Open to Tourism: guided tours, tastings, wine sales, and gastronomic services.
